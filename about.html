About Me

Caroline Eggett

I am a third year marketing student, with minors in business analytics and studio art. I like to be able to use data to create pieces of art outside of my photography hobby. Feel free to look on my Github or reach out to my via email to schedule a time for a shoot!


I am a thriving photographer in the state of Ohio. I love to snap photos of people to capture moments of time that drift away endlessly like a lost boat at sea. I have been doing photography for about a year, but I can assure you that I have experience! My camera may not be top knotch, but the camera performance is not what makes the picture good... it is the photographer taking the picture.

I love to read and eat chocolate. I prefer to relax outside in the warm summer weather than be cozy in a blanket in the cold winter. My favorite times of the year are summer and fall. I love sunflowers and falling leaves.

I can do a bunch of types of photography: senior pictures, family portraits, head shots, and whatever special event you have in mind.

As a college student, I hope to bring a new perception to the art of photography. I have never taken a photography class, but have used trial and error as my teacher. I am always watching YouTube videos to become the best photographer I can be. I won't try to disappoint in my abilities.
